Jeweller Profile:

Musee Roo

📍Bristol, United Kingdom

💡Historical jewellery for the conscious antiquarian

Roo started her business from the dregs of a student overdraft and from her university bedroom in Bristol, whilst studying for a 4-year integrated MArts in History with Innovation at the University of Bristol.

She finds most of her inspiration in museums, and wants to make many more jewels inspired by original artefacts. Roo has many wax sculptures to get cast, and wants to make ancient treasures wearable, and bring them into the everyday.
